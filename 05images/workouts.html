Getting Starting with Working Out

When starting on building your workout routine, find what would work best for you! below are good starting place when begining to workout. Find what will fit for you and build your routine!

Calisthenices

Calisthenices is a form of resistance training that uses the weight of your body along with gravity to strengthen muscles and increase endurance

Weight Training

Weight training is a type of exercise that uses free weights to build strength. Unlike bodyweight exercises which use your own bodyweight to build muscle, weight training exercises use barbells, dumbbells, kettlebells, or weight machines to increase muscular strength throughout the body.

>

Stability and Mobility Training

Stability training is a form of exercise that focuses on improving the body's ability to maintain balance and control movement. This type of training targets the body's core muscles, which include the abdominals, obliques, lower back, and hip muscles. The goal of stability training is to improve the body's ability to maintain stability during movement and reduce the risk of injury.

>

Cardio

Cardio, Short for cardiovascular training, and is any exercise—such as running, cycling, or dancing—that elevates your heart rate. Cardio is also considered aerobic exercise, meaning it demands elevated oxygen flow, which causes you to breathe harder. Cardio is essential if you want to burn calories, strengthen your heart and lungs, and be healthier overall.below is a link how to start running.
